actually drinks this stuff.

Dr. Juice started with a simple, stubborn idea: that a juice should be made of fruit and vegetables — and nothing else.

No added sugar. No syrups. No water to stretch it, no ice to water it down, no chemicals to keep it on a shelf for a month. We make everything fresh, in-house, every single day.

What began as one Glendale counter has become a neighborhood habit — regulars we know by name, workouts fueled, mornings started right. We're family-owned, and it shows in every bottle.

Come in hungry. Leave better.
